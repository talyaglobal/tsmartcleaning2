# NGO/Recruiting Agency Registration Form - Complete Prompt

## Main Overview Prompt

```
I need to create a comprehensive registration and application form for NGOs and 
recruiting agencies who want to partner with tSmartCleaning to provide employment 
opportunities to people seeking immediate income through respectable cleaning jobs 
in USA and Canada.

**PURPOSE:**
NGOs/agencies help vulnerable populations (immigrants, refugees, single parents, 
unemployed, low-income individuals) access dignified, immediate employment in the 
cleaning industry while earning fair wages.

**DUAL FORM SYSTEM:**
1. NGO/Agency Registration Form (to become an approved partner)
2. Candidate Submission Portal (to submit candidates for job placement)

**TARGET USERS:**
- Non-profit organizations
- Community service agencies
- Immigrant support organizations
- Women's shelters and support groups
- Refugee resettlement agencies
- Employment training programs
- Faith-based community organizations

**KEY OBJECTIVES:**
- Fast-track employment for people needing immediate income
- Provide dignified work with fair pay
- Support vulnerable populations
- Create sustainable partnerships with vetted NGOs
- Track placement success and impact

Please help me create this comprehensive dual-form system.
```
