Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about booking, service, and support.

How can I schedule a cleaning?

Book online by choosing your service, selecting a date, and confirming your details. Our team will take care of the rest.

Which locations do you cover?

We serve multiple US cities. Enter your zip code during booking to check if we operate in your area.

Is rescheduling or cancellation possible?

You can reschedule or cancel through your account dashboard. Please review our policy for details on timing and fees.

Who provides the cleaning services?

Our professionals are background-checked, trained, and experienced. We work with both employees and vetted freelancers.
