## Customer Guide

### Getting Started

#### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the signup page
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Fill in your personal information
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile

#### Setting Up Your Profile

1. Go to **Profile** settings
2. Add your phone number
3. Upload a profile photo (optional)
4. Set your preferences

### Booking a Service

#### Step 1: Choose a Service

1. Click **"Book Now"** or navigate to booking page
2. Select service type:
   - Residential cleaning
   - Commercial cleaning
   - Deep cleaning
   - Move-in/out cleaning
   - Post-construction cleaning
   - Window cleaning
   - Carpet cleaning
   - Eco-friendly cleaning

#### Step 2: Select Date and Time

1. Choose your preferred date from the calendar
2. Select an available time slot
3. Confirm your selection

#### Step 3: Add Service Address

1. Enter your address
2. Add any special instructions
3. Save the address for future bookings

#### Step 4: Add Extras (Optional)

- Additional rooms
- Window cleaning
- Oven cleaning
- Refrigerator cleaning
- Inside cabinets
- Baseboards
- Inside windows

#### Step 5: Review and Confirm

1. Review your booking details
2. Check pricing breakdown
3. Apply loyalty points (if available)
4. Confirm and pay

### Managing Bookings

#### Viewing Your Bookings

1. Go to **My Bookings**
2. View upcoming, past, and cancelled bookings
3. Filter by status or date

#### Rescheduling a Booking

1. Open the booking you want to reschedule
2. Click **"Reschedule"**
3. Select a new date and time
4. Confirm the changes

#### Cancelling a Booking

1. Open the booking you want to cancel
2. Click **"Cancel Booking"**
3. Select a cancellation reason
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Review refund policy

#### Recurring Bookings

1. When booking, select **"Make this recurring"**
2. Choose frequency:
   - Weekly
   - Bi-weekly
   - Monthly
3. Set number of occurrences
4. Confirm recurring booking

### Payment Methods

#### Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Payment Methods**
2. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
3. Enter card details
4. Save for future use

#### Managing Payment Methods

- Set default payment method
- Update card information
- Remove payment methods

### Loyalty Program

#### Earning Points

- Complete bookings: 100 points per booking
- Refer friends: 500 points per referral
- Leave reviews: 50 points per review
- Achieve milestones: Bonus points

#### Redeeming Points

1. Go to **Loyalty** section
2. View available rewards
3. Select reward to redeem
4. Points are automatically applied

#### Checking Balance

- View points balance in dashboard
- See transaction history
- Track progress to next tier

### Reviews and Ratings

#### Leaving a Review

1. After service completion, you'll receive an email
2. Click **"Leave a Review"**
3. Rate the service (1-5 stars)
4. Write a detailed review
5. Submit your review

#### Editing Reviews

1. Go to **My Reviews**
2. Find the review you want to edit
3. Click **"Edit"**
4. Update your rating or comments
5. Save changes

### Finding Cleaners

#### Using the Directory

1. Navigate to **Find Cleaners**
2. Browse available cleaning companies
3. Use filters:
   - Location
   - Rating
   - Price range
   - Services offered
   - Verified status
4. View company profiles
5. Read reviews
6. Book directly with a company

#### Search Features

- Search by company name
- Filter by location (with map view)
- Sort by rating, price, distance
- Save favorite companies

### Insurance

#### Viewing Your Policy

1. Go to **Insurance** section
2. View active policies
3. Check coverage details
4. Download certificate

#### Filing a Claim

1. Go to **Insurance** → **File a Claim**
2. Select the policy
3. Enter incident details:
   - Date of incident
   - Description
   - Amount claimed
4. Upload supporting documents
5. Submit claim
6. Track claim status

### Support

#### Getting Help

1. Visit **Contact** page
2. Fill out contact form
3. Or use live chat (if available)
4. Check FAQ section

#### Common Issues

**Booking Issues:**
- No available time slots: Try different dates
- Payment failed: Check payment method
- Booking not confirmed: Check email for confirmation

**Account Issues:**
- Can't log in: Use password reset
- Email not verified: Check spam folder
- Profile not updating: Clear cache and try again

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

**Q: Can I cancel a booking?**
A: Yes, you can cancel bookings up to 24 hours before the scheduled time.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept all major credit cards and debit cards.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, all payments are processed securely through Stripe.

### Booking Questions

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**
A: You can book up to 3 months in advance.

**Q: Can I reschedule a booking?**
A: Yes, you can reschedule up to 24 hours before the scheduled time.

**Q: What if I need to cancel last minute?**
A: Last-minute cancellations may be subject to fees. Check cancellation policy.

**Q: Do you offer same-day service?**
A: Same-day service is available based on provider availability.

### Payment Questions

**Q: When will I be charged?**
A: You'll be charged when you confirm your booking.

**Q: What is your refund policy?**
A: Refunds are processed according to our cancellation policy.

**Q: Can I use multiple payment methods?**
A: Currently, one payment method per booking is supported.

### Provider Questions

**Q: How do I become a verified provider?**
A: Complete your profile, submit verification documents, and pass background check.

**Q: How often do I get paid?**
A: Payouts are processed weekly by default.

**Q: What fees do you charge providers?**
A: Platform fees vary by service type. Check provider agreement.

### Support

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Use the contact form, email support, or check the help center.

**Q: What are your support hours?**
A: Support is available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST.
